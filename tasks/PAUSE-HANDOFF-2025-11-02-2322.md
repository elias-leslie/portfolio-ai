## Key Decisions

**Architecture**:
- **Caching Strategy**: Different TTLs based on data volatility
  - Fundamentals: 24 hours (slow-changing)
  - News: 6 hours (fast-changing)
  - Earnings: 30 days (very slow-changing)
- **Calculator Design**: Dual-method approach (ATR + technical)
  - Stop loss: Use tighter of ATR-based or swing low
  - Profit target: Use higher of ATR-based or swing high

**Patterns Established**:
- Use `reference_cache` table for all external API caching
- Date-based TTL for slow data (fundamentals/earnings)
- Timestamp-based TTL for fast data (news)
- Cache None results to prevent repeated failed lookups
